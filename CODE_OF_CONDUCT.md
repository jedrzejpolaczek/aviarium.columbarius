# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, trolling, or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behaviour may be reported to **jedrzej.polaczek@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate.
